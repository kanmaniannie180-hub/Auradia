# 🌟 Auradia - Emotional Wellbeing & Decision Intelligence Platform

## 📖 Overview

Auradia is an innovative emotional wellbeing platform designed to help users understand their emotions, identify behavioral patterns, reflect on personal growth, and make informed decisions through intelligent guidance.

The platform combines emotion tracking, voice-based reflection, personalized insights, and decision-support systems into a single connected ecosystem that promotes emotional awareness and self-improvement.

⚠️ **Project Status:** Ongoing Development

Current Progress:

* ✅ UI/UX Design Completed
* ✅ Frontend Prototype Developed
* 🚧 Backend Development In Progress
* 🚧 Firebase Integration In Progress
* 🚧 AI & Voice Processing Modules In Progress
* 🚧 PWA Conversion Planned

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Many individuals struggle with:

* Emotional inconsistency
* Stress and anxiety
* Lack of self-awareness
* Impulsive decision-making
* Limited access to personalized emotional support

Existing solutions often provide generic recommendations without understanding individual emotional patterns.

Auradia aims to bridge this gap through a personalized emotional intelligence ecosystem.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🫧 Mood Bubbles

Interactive mood tracking using visual emotion bubbles and intensity levels.

### 🗺️ Emotional Map

Visualizes emotional patterns, trends, and behavioral changes over time.

### 🔮 Time Capsule

Store messages, images, and voice notes for future reflection and growth tracking.

### 🎙️ Voice Emotion Journal

Record voice reflections and analyze emotional patterns through speech.

### 🌊 Ripple Effects Engine

Predicts possible positive and negative outcomes of emotional decisions before taking action.

### 🤖 AI Mood Coach

Provides personalized emotional guidance and supportive recommendations.

### 🌿 Calm Zone

Guided breathing exercises and relaxation experiences with optional ambient sounds.

### ⚠️ Smart Triggers

Detects emotional fluctuations and recommends appropriate support actions.

### 💬 Nudge AI

Delivers personalized nudges and motivational guidance based on emotional history and goals.

### 🙏 Gratitude & Reflection System

Encourages positive thinking and self-reflection through gratitude journaling.

### 🔒 Secure Authentication

Email Login, Google Sign-In, and Two-Factor Authentication support.
